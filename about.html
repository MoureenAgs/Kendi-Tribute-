About Kendi

Kendi Ntwiga is a trailblazer in the African tech space, known for her leadership in some of the world’s top tech companies including Microsoft, HP, and IBM. Her influence goes beyond technology—she's a passionate advocate for women in STEM and a strong believer in the power of mentorship.

Her Achievements

  • Former Country Manager for Microsoft Kenya
  • Leader in various global tech firms such as HP, Check Point, and IBM
  • Founder of She-Goes-Tech, a platform that mentors young girls and women into tech careers
  • Recognized among the Top 40 Under 40 Women in Kenya

Legacy and Inspiration

Kendi’s work continues to inspire a new generation of young Africans to pursue careers in technology and leadership. Her journey reminds us that with vision, integrity, and determination, barriers can be broken, and meaningful change can be achieved.
